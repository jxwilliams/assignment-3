Quick Tips

Watch for student discounts and lunch specials. Lines are longest 12–1:30pm, so mobile order when you can.

What this mini‑blog is about

Welcome to Campus Eats, a short guide to places within a quick walk of Petty. I picked spots that are affordable and easy for students. Each place lists three specific items so you can decide fast.

Tate Street Coffee House

344 Tate st · Sandwiches & Coffee

small coffee shop, good setting to study in

Avocado Breakfast Bagel — Creamy and filling before class.

Chicken Pesto Panini — Hot, melty, and fast.

Iced Caramel Latte — Sweet but balanced.

Crazy Mexico

332 Tate St· Tacos & Bowls

Quick service with build‑your‑own options.

Chicken Street Tacos — Simple and flavorful.

Veggie Burrito Bowl — Good vegetarian option.

Chips & Queso — Shareable side.

Ghong Chai

344 Tate st · Boba & Desserts

Good for an afternoon pick‑me‑up; many flavors and sweetness levels.

Brown Sugar Boba — Chewy pearls, caramel notes.

Matcha Latte — Try 50% sweetness.

Taiyaki Ice Cream — Warm cone with soft‑serve.
